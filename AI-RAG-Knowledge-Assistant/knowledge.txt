Endee.io is a platform focused on vector search and artificial intelligence infrastructure.

Vector search finds information based on meaning rather than exact keyword matches by using numerical representations called embeddings.

Embeddings convert text into high-dimensional vectors that capture semantic relationships between pieces of information.

Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) is an AI technique that combines information retrieval with language model generation to produce more accurate answers.

RAG systems first retrieve relevant context from a knowledge base and then use a large language model to generate a response.

Vector databases store embeddings and allow fast similarity search across large datasets.

Semantic search improves information discovery by understanding the intent behind a user's question rather than relying only on keywords.
